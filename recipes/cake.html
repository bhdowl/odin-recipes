Chocolate Cherry Dump Cake

Doesn't that cake just look gorgeous?

This cake is the perfect recipe for when you need something quick and simple. With only 3 ingredients, the Chocolate Cherry Dump Cake will albsolutely blow your mind.

I won't bore you with any life stories or personal anecdotes. Let's getBAKED!

Ingredients

  • 2 twenty-one oz cans cherry pie filling
  • 1 box Betty Crocker Super Moist Devil's Food Cake Mix
  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • top with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees and grease a 13x9inch glass baking dish.
  2. Spread pie filling in baking dish.
  3. Top with dry cake mix; gently shake pan to distribute evenly.
  4. Pour melted butter over top, tilting pan to cover with as much butter as possible.
  5. Bake 42 to 45 minutes or until mostly dry on top and bubbly around the edges. Cool 10 minutes before serving.
