Mama's Famous Lasagna

Tasty, easy, and, utterly delicious.

Are you looking for the perfect lasagna recipe? Well look no further! I have exactly what you are looking for.

Sometimes you want a family dinner to be a little bit special. You can make that happen with this quick and easy lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28 ounce can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 6 ounce can of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and assemble all ingredients.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes.
